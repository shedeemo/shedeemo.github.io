About me

I am a PhD candidate at Ohio State University specializing in comparative politics and research methods. I am originally from Jersey City, New Jersey (a.k.a. the greatest city in the world) but moved to Columbus, Ohio for graduate school. Substantively, I'm interested in political trust and electoral politics in authoritarian contexts, with a regional focus on the Middle East. Methodologically, I am interested in survey experiments, fraud forensics, and issues of coneptualization and measurement. When I'm not reading or coding, I enjoy playing video games, binging low quality Netflix shows, and camping.
